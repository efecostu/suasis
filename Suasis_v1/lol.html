About US

Shortly About US

SUASIS is a globally-oriented high-tech company, offering solutions in the areas of Underwater Acoustics, IT, Real-Time Systems Data Acquisition Systems and new underwater sensor development. For more than 10 years, SUASIS has been developing Technologies and engineering solutions in Turkey, using over 45 years of experience of its founders and providing systems for underwater applicatıons both in Europe, USA and in Far-East. SUASIS has a well established experience and know-how, with special infrastructures for Thin Line and Cardioid Towed Arrays and Vertical Line Array design and production. SUASIS has been actively working on the development of vector sensors which is developed under EC EUROSTARS project. Currently new vector sensors are being produced for low-frequency applications. SUASIS is also involved with two EC FP7 projects for underwater communication (SUNRISE) and Underwater sensors for ROVs (Hexaterra). Under a TUBITAK 1511 project SUASIS has been working on the development of the new generation underwater modems based on OFDM (Orthogonal Frequency Division Multiplexing) in collaboration with Kadir Has University and Özyeğin Universit SUASIS has been implementing as payloads the newly developed vector sensors and thin-line array to the ın-house experimental AUV.
